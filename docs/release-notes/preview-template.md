## Known limitations

- GlucoDesk is a preview app.
- GlucoDesk is an awareness companion, not a medical device.
- Do not use it for treatment or insulin dosing decisions.

## Safety disclaimer

GlucoDesk is an awareness companion. It is not a medical device and must not be used for treatment or insulin dosing decisions.

Always use official CGM apps, medical devices, and clinician guidance for treatment decisions.

## Manual smoke test

Before publishing this release, verify:

1. App starts on macOS.
2. Dashboard loads without crashing.
3. Menu bar indicator appears.
4. Presence panel opens from menu bar.
5. Privacy mode Off shows glucose value and glycemic menu bar icon.
6. Privacy mode On hides glucose value and shows the neutral blue privacy icon.
7. Privacy mode persists after restart.
8. Mock provider works.
9. Nightscout provider error states are understandable.
10. App quits cleanly from the presence panel.
